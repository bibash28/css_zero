About Us

Yalax Hotel

Yalax Hotel is a newly launched hotel in Nepal located in the heart of Kathmandu. It offers modern and comfortable accommodation with 48 stylishly decorated rooms equipped with all necessary amenities such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, private bathrooms, and free Wi-Fi.The hotel boasts of its rooftop restaurant, which offers a stunning view of the surrounding hills and the city skyline. Guests can enjoy delicious local and international cuisine while enjoying the panoramic view.
